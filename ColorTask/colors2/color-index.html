Here I am using only two colors: white and a light blue (with a tint of violet).

This was actually the first design I did. For me it was easier to do this one with only two colors, three colors was more complicated for me.

The approach I had was to divide elements with one or the other color. Text would preferably be white and background would be blue, but on buttons it will be reversed to highlight them and show that they are different.

Main content area is bordered with white line to show that it is separated from the navigation bar and the side bar.
